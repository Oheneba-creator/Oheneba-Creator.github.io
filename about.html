Welcome to The Nelson Foundation

Empowering disabled females to thrive and succeed.

Together, we create opportunities for women with disabilities to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Our Vision: To build a world where every woman, regardless of her physical abilities, has equal opportunities to succeed and contribute meaningfully to society.

Join Us

Who We Are

At The Nelson Foundation, we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of disabled females. Our mission is to provide support, resources, and opportunities to empower women with disabilities, helping them lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Our mission

We believe that every woman, regardless of her physical abilities, deserves equal opportunities to thrive and succeed. Our foundation focuses on creating inclusive environments, offering educational programs, and advocating for the rights of disabled females.

What We Do

  • Support Services: We offer a range of support services, including counseling, career guidance, and personal development workshops, to help disabled women overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
  • Education and Training: Our educational initiatives provide access to quality education and vocational training, enabling women to acquire new skills and enhance their employability.
  • Advocacy: We work tirelessly to raise awareness about the issues faced by disabled females and advocate for policies that promote inclusivity and accessibility in all aspects of society.
  • Community Building: We foster a strong sense of community through events, support groups, and online platforms, where women can connect, share experiences, and support each other.

Our Impact

Since our inception, The Nelson Foundation has positively impacted the lives of numerous disabled females. We have seen women gain confidence, secure employment, and build meaningful relationships, all while contributing to their communities.
